Week 48, 2025

Smarter Posting Times and prepping for Customer Experience Week

After a few weeks focusing on refactors, this week we have been working on improvements to our Posting Times experience while squashing some sneaky bugs along the way. Built several improvements to help users set up their posting schedules faster, including copying the posting schedule from another channel and previewing schedule changes. Also fixed three bugs and removed unused queues for the legacy web workers. Plus, we're prepping for Customer Experience Week (December 15–18) where the whole company focuses on low-risk, high-impact improvements.
